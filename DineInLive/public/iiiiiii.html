Powerful Features That Make A Difference

Our comprehensive mess management system is designed to address all aspects of mess operations, from attendance tracking to feedback collection.

Smart Attendance Tracking

Automate attendance with QR scanning, facial recognition, or manual entry. Generate reports and analyze patterns for better resource planning.

Meal Planning & Menu Management

Create balanced meal plans, manage rotating menus, track nutrition, and accommodate dietary restrictions with ease.

Automated Billing
